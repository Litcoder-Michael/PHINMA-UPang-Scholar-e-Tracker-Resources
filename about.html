PHINMA UPang Scholar e-Tracker


Upang Scholar e-Tracker is an application that converts the traditional way (paper-based) of recording data to a digital format. It was design for the students of PHINMA University of Pangasinan Scholars which aims to the following:

Provide efficiency, as the use of paper was already forbidden, ease of use exeplified as misplacement due to tons of paper works or at worst missing, tracking their day to day activity, more helpful way to earn their scholarship renewal and most importantly, data's are always on the go, they can access if anytime and anywhere to check their progress and fulfill their shortcomings.
